Chiang Mai, Thailand

Chiang Mai is a favorite destination for digital nomads thanks to its peaceful atmosphere, low cost of living, and abundance of cafes and co-working spaces. It offers a blend of traditional Thai culture and modern amenities, making it a perfect balance for remote work and exploration.

Highlights for Digital Nomads:

  • Extremely affordable lifestyle
  • Fast and reliable internet
  • Friendly local and expat community
  • Safe and walkable city
  • Great local cuisine and street food
