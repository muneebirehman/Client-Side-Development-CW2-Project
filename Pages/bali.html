Why Bali?

Bali is a top digital nomad destination thanks to its affordable lifestyle, tropical climate, and supportive coworking culture. Ubud and Canggu are hubs for remote workers offering beautiful surroundings and vibrant communities.

Nomad Highlights

  • High-speed Wi-Fi and coworking spaces like Dojo Bali and Hubud
  • Affordable accommodations and local food
  • Friendly visa options for longer stays
  • Activities from surfing to yoga retreats
