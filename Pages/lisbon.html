Why Lisbon?

Lisbon, the capital of Portugal, is a thriving hotspot for digital nomads. With beautiful weather, fast internet, a vibrant culture, and an ever-growing community of remote workers, Lisbon is the perfect destination for combining work with travel.

Highlights for Digital Nomads:

  • Abundant coworking spaces and cafes
  • Affordable public transport
  • Access to beaches and historical sites
  • Great food and wine culture
